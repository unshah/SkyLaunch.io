# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 26, 2024  
**Last Updated:** December 26, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to SkyLaunch ("we," "our," or "us"). SkyLaunch is a mobile application designed to help student pilots and Certified Flight Instructors (CFIs) manage their flight training journey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using SkyLaunch, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information**
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Full name
- User role (student pilot or CFI)

**Profile Information**
- Training goal (Private, Instrument, Commercial, or ATP)
- Weekly training hours preference
- Schedule intensity preference (aggressive, balanced, relaxed)
- Current flight hours
- Target completion date
- Home airport (ICAO code, e.g., "KFFZ")

**Flight Log Data**
- Flight dates
- Departure and arrival airports
- Flight duration
- Instructor name
- Aircraft type and registration
- Flight conditions (VFR, IFR, night)
- Solo and cross-country flight indicators
- Flight notes

**Training Progress Data**
- Training task completion status
- Scheduled and completed training dates
- Task notes

**CFI-Specific Information** (if applicable)
- CFI certificate number
- Certificate expiry date
- Instructor ratings (CFI, CFII, MEI)
- Student-CFI relationships
- Maneuver grades and notes
- Endorsement records

## Information Collected Automatically

### Usage Data
We may collect information about how you access and use the application, including:
- Device type and operating system
- App usage patterns
- Error logs and crash reports

### Third-Party API Data
SkyLaunch integrates with the following third-party services to provide weather and airport information:

**AviationWeather.gov**
- We fetch METAR (meteorological aerodrome reports) data based on your home airport to provide real-time weather information and flight recommendations.

**AirportDB**
- We retrieve runway and airport information based on your home airport ICAO code to calculate crosswind components and training suitability.

We do not share your personal information with these services. Only your home airport ICAO code is used to fetch publicly available aviation data.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide and maintain the app** — Enable core features like scheduling, progress tracking, and weather integration
- **Personalize your experience** — Customize training schedules based on your goals and preferences
- **Generate weather-aware recommendations** — Determine optimal flying days based on current weather conditions
- **Track training progress** — Monitor and visualize your journey toward pilot certification
- **Facilitate CFI-student relationships** — Enable instructors to manage and grade students
- **Improve our services** — Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality
- **Communicate with you** — Send important updates about your account or the app

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
Your data is stored securely using **Supabase**, a secure backend-as-a-service platform that uses:
- **PostgreSQL database** with row-level security (RLS) policies
- **Industry-standard encryption** for data at rest and in transit
- **Secure authentication** via Supabase Auth

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures, including:
- Encrypted password storage
- Row-level security ensuring users can only access their own data
- Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
- Regular security updates and monitoring

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing
We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

**With Your Consent**

- We use Supabase for backend services, which processes data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements

**Legal Requirements**
- We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, or legal process

- We may disclose information to protect the safety, rights, or property of SkyLaunch, our users, or the public
